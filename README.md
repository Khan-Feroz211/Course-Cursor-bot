# 🎓 Prof. AI Assistant

## What This Does
This app helps you store all your course files in one place and ask questions in plain English.  
It gives answers from your own uploaded files and also creates graphs from your instructions.

## ▶️ Starting the App
1. Double-click `Launch_Windows.bat`.
2. Wait for browser to open (~10 seconds).
3. Done! ✅  
Note: First time takes 2-3 minutes for setup.

## Adding Your Course Files
1. Open **📁 My Documents**.
2. Drag and drop files into the upload box (or click to select files).
3. Wait until each file shows ✅ Indexed.
4. Your files are now ready for AI questions.

## Asking Questions
1. Open **🤖 Ask AI**.
2. Type your question and press **Send**.
3. Read the answer and source references below it.

Example questions:
- "What are the assumptions of Euler-Bernoulli beam theory in my notes?"
- "From my uploaded material, explain factors affecting concrete compressive strength."
- "What load-deflection relationship is shown in the lab sheet?"

## Stopping the App
Close the black window.  
That will stop the app.
